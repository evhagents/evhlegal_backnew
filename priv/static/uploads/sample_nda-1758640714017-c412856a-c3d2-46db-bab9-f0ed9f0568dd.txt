NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement (this "Agreement") is entered into on January 15, 2024 (the "Effective Date") by and between:

DISCLOSING PARTY: TechCorp Inc., a Delaware corporation
RECEIVING PARTY: StartupCo LLC, a California limited liability company

DEFINITIONS
"Confidential Information" means all non-public, proprietary information disclosed by the Disclosing Party to the Receiving Party.

CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
The Disclosing Party may disclose technical specifications, business plans, financial information, and other proprietary data to the Receiving Party.

EXCLUSIONS
The following information shall not be considered Confidential Information:
(a) Information that is publicly available
(b) Information independently developed by the Receiving Party

OBLIGATIONS
The Receiving Party agrees to:
1. Maintain confidentiality of all Confidential Information
2. Use the information solely for evaluation purposes
3. Not disclose to third parties without written consent

TERM
This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of three (3) years from the Effective Date.

RETURN OF MATERIALS
Upon termination, the Receiving Party shall return or destroy all Confidential Information.

REMEDIES
The Disclosing Party may seek injunctive relief for any breach of this Agreement.

GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

MISCELLANEOUS
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
